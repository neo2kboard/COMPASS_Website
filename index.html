ABOUT COMPASS

COMPASS, also known as the CompSci Association, is a dynamic academic club composed of Computer Science students. The club fosters collaboration and innovation, providing its members with insights into the vital role technology plays in today's development and progress. Through various activities and initiatives, COMPASS empowers students to explore and understand the impact of technology on our rapidly evolving world.
